EDUCATIONAL VIDEO CENTER

The Educational Video Center teaches young people how to produce documentary films to create social impact. Due to a previous outdated website, I transferred their website from Drupal to WordPress. I designed and developed their new website (HTML, CSS, PHP, eCommerce, responsive design).

  • Client: Educational Video Center
  • Date: October 2015
  • Service: Web Design

AAANY

The Arab American Association of New York supports and empowers the Arab American and Arab immigrant community in NYC. At first I was asked to implement an online volunteer application form; however, I ended up revamping and transferring their website from WordPress to Squarespace since they wanted a platform that was easy to use and low-maintenance. I designed their new website using a pre-made Squarespace template and added my personal injections using HTML and CSS.

  • Client: Arab American Association of NY
  • Date: May 2014
  • Service: Web Design

INTERACTIVE TIMELINE

As a part of the Educational Video Center's 30th anniversary marketing campaign, I created an interactive timeline that features the works and legacy of the organization using an open-source tool, TimelineJS and added customizations using HTML and CSS.

  • Client: Educational Video Center
  • Date: June 2015
  • Service: Interactive Media

VOLUNTEER MARKETING MATERIALS

As the Arab American Association of New York's former Youth Services Coordinator, I was responsible for the recruitment of volunteers as well as the facilitation of the organization's after-school tutoring program, Kitaab Club. As an effort to engage and orient volunteers, I created a volunteer brochure along with a tutors guide using Adobe Photoshop and Indesign.

  • Client: Arab American Association of NY
  • Date: April 2014
  • Service: Print Design
